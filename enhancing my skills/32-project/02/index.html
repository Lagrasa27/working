Our Story

The Green Thumbs Gardening Club was founded in 2015 by a small group of local residents who shared a common love for growing things. What started as casual meetups in backyards quickly blossomed into a thriving community dedicated to sustainable gardening practices, urban farming, and beautifying our neighborhood.

We host workshops, plant swaps, community garden projects, and educational sessions covering everything from composting to advanced horticulture. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting out, you'll find a welcoming environment and plenty of opportunities to grow with us.
